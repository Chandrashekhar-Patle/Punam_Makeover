From South to North- Different Indian Bridal Makeup Looks That You Must Know as A Bride!

  • Posted By : Saloni
  • Last Updated : May 03,2021
  • Post View : 68,956
  • Comments : 1

While searching for the perfect bridal outfit for your wedding, do you think about your traditional makeup look that you will opt for the wedding day? As a bride, you should think about it that what type of traditional Indian bridal makeup look will augment your inner beauty!!  

In India, our nation has a culturally diverse landscape at every step with various types of bridal makeup looks, wedding styles, and bridal attires in their light. A big fat Indian wedding can define by sumptuous jewelry and striking bridal fashions along with the astonished look of a bride.

The idea behind the Indian makeup look is to stand out from your regular makeup look and bring out your inner diva effortlessly. The diverse number of cultures within our nation calls for different bridal makeup looks for each type of wedding.

As we know each culture of India has its different look when it is about a bridal look. While opting for a bridal look for the wedding, brides should keep in mind that it should represent their personality and enhance their charm.

In India, we can see the contrast in bridal outfits, bridal makeup, and wedding beauty in their way. We came across various amazing traditional Indian makeup looks. Here are some stunning pictures that will make you believe that divas still exist.

Without any further ado, here we have brought out some top picks of traditional bridal looks and we are head over heels in love with these loos, have a look at them-

Traditional Bridal Makeup Looks That Will Leave You Astonished

For a bride, it is important to pick a perfect bridal look for her wedding. While selecting a bridal makeup look it is essential to take time for thinking what you want. Whether you want a South Indian makeup look or Christian bridal look, give attention to each look you are opting for your wedding. Be A Serene Beauty with This Sultry Traditional South Indian Bridal Makeup

Just like we love the south Indian cuisine, south Indian bridal makeup look also gives us a reason to love it due to its simplicity and added charm in it. Baggy garlands of jasmines plated with elaborated braids, fresh and bright-hued skills, and heavy gold jewelry are the main aspects of a traditional south Indian makeup look, but if you want to increase the various elements of this elegant beauty, then try bronzed perfection.

A Magnificent Bengali Traditional Makeup Look

When we talk about a Bengali beauty, a bold, beautiful, and attention-grabbing look comes in our mind automatically. A ton of blush, bold red lipstick, bronze-hued eyeshadow, winged eyeliner, luscious eyelashes, and well-defined eyebrows are the famous aspects of a Bengali bride.

Traditional Elegance in Kerala Bridal Makeup Look

This Kerala bride looks splendid in her traditional avatar wearing a white sari with amazing gold Zari border. This vibrant orange saree is very popular among Kerala brides with beautiful golden Zari.

You can do hair adorned in white jasmines and a bindi on your face will add a sparkle in your bridal look as it will look so clean and simplistic.

Classy and Sassy Traditional Makeup Look of a Punjabi Bride

boisterous, loud, and funny- these are the signs of a Punjabi bride!! These Punjabi brides are all dressed up in a vibrant red-orange, fuschias, dark reds, velvety greens, and mustards, etc. are the famous color for this region’s bride.
