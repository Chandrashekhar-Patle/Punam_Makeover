BEST BRIDAL MAKEUP ARTIST IN BHANDARA-GONDIA

PUNAM was born & brought up in Gondia, has a degree in B.Sc in Computer Science from Gondwana University in Gadchiroli. She recognized her passion for makeup in 2021. She always plumped for makeup during her college days. she leaned towards this field and then started her journey as makeup artist. And that makes her very experienced in this industry. The transformation that was brought after every look and hairstyles in that short term caught her fascination. She knew she wanted to be the Bridal Makeup Artist in Bhandara….Hard work, dedication and zest soon brought her up to the top as a bridal makeup artist and makeup educator.
